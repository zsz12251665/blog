Solution

This problem is just a mathmatical problem. The speed how fast the two armies get close is (#u#+#v#), the sum of their speeds. Then the time they meet will be /#l#/#u#+#v#/. The reporter is always running, so the time he runs is also /#l#/#u#+#v#/. And the distance he runs will equal the speed multiply the time that is /#w##l#/#u#+#v#/. So when you read a group of (#u#,#v#,#w#,#l#), just output /#w##l#/#u#+#v#/.
