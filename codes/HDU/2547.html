Similar to HDU 2548, this problem is a mathmatical problem too. f(#x#,#y#,#m#,#n#)=(\#x#(^2^)+#y#(^2^)+#m#(^2^)+#n#(^2^)-2#m##x#-2#n##y#\)=(\(#x#-#m#)(^2^)+(#y#-#n#)(^2^)\), which refers to the distance between (#x#,#y#) and (#m#,#n#). So when the hurt is reduced to mininum, the sum of the distances is reduced to minium too. It equals the distance between (#a#,#b#) and (#c#,#d#), which will be (\(#a#-#c#)(^2^)+(#b#-#d#)(^2^)\).
