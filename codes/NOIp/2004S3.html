Solution

First, to every v{i} in o{[}c{1}o{,}v{n}o{]}, we need to find the longest ascending subsequence from 1 to v{i} and the longest descending subsequence from v{i} to v{n}. Both of them must include v{i}. In this way, we can count the number of the students who get out when v{i} is the tallest student in the team. And then, we can search the o{[}c{1}o{,}v{n}o{]} to get the smallest answer.
