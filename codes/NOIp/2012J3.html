Solution

This problem is a dp problem. We make the v{dp} array that v{dp}b{v{i}o{,}v{j}} means after choosing v{i} kinds of flowers we know have v{j} pots of flowers. And the state transition formula will be v{dp}b{v{i}o{,}v{j}}o{=}o{∑}i{o{min}o{(}v{a}b{v{i}}o{,}v{j}o{)}l{}v{k}o{=}c{0}}v{dp}b{v{i}o{-}c{1}o{,}v{j}o{-}v{k}}, that v{k} means to the number of the pots of the v{i}-th flowers. And the answer, of course, will be v{dp}b{v{n}o{,}v{m}}.
