Solution

This problem is a dp problem. We make the #dp# array that #dp#(_#i#,#j#_) means after choosing #i# kinds of flowers we know have #j# pots of flowers. And the state transition formula will be #dp#(_#i#,#j#_)=∑(_#k#∈[0,min(#a#(_#i#_),#j#)]_)(#dp#(_#i#-1,#j#-#k#_)), that #k# means to the number of the pots of the #i#-th flowers. And the answer, of course, will be #dp#(_#n#,#m#_).
