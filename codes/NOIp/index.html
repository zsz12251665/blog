National Olympiad in Informatics in provinces

Introduction

National Olympiad in Informatics in provinces(abbr. NOIp) is an informatic competition which is held from 1995 for Chinese secondary school students(including Hong Kong and Macao). The competitors will be devided in 2 groups. The PJ group is mainly for junior high school students, and the TG group is mainly for senior high school students. The preliminary is written test. Only the top 10%~15% of the competitors will be able to attend the final. The final is programming test. For the TG group before 2011 and the PJ group, the final was held in a single day with 4 problems for competitors to solve. From 2011, it is held in the whole weekend with 3 problems each day for TG group.
