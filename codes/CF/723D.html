Solution

This problem required us to find out all the lakes, and according to the greedy algorithm, we will fill the smallest lakes with land until there are only v{k} of them left. But the problem is how to find out all the lakes. The answer is DFS. What's more, you'll find the data range is just c{1}o{≤}v{n}o{,}v{m}o{≤}c{50} and c{0}o{≤}v{k}o{≤}c{50}. So what else do we need to worry about? The simulation is very enough.
