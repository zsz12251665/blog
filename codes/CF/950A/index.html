Solution

The problem is very simple. As you have seen , you can even use a BF to solve the problem just like Solution 1. You decide which hand each ambidexter should use to maximize the number of the players.

However, you can also solve the problem in as Solution 2 does. Suppose that there are more right-handers than left-handers (actually there are). If there are more right-handers than the other two, we could only have twice number of left-handers and ambidexters. Otherwise we could split the rest of ambidexters into two halves to enlarge the two groups.
