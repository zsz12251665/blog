And if I wanted the v{q}-th one, the answer would be the o{(}v{n}o{+}f{v{q}l{}c{2}}o{)}-th one. What's more, the time complexity of the algorithm is o{O}o{(}o{lg}v{n}o{)}. So the problem is solved.
